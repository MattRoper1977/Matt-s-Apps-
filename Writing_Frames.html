How to use Writing Frames Studio

  1. Pick a frame that matches what you're writing — a recount, a story, a persuasive piece, a science method, and more.
  2. Each box is one part of your writing. Read the hint, and tap a sentence starter to drop it straight in — then keep going in your own words.
  3. The word bank gives useful words to tap in too.
  4. Your finished writing builds up at the bottom as you go. 📋 Copy or ⬇ Save it when you're done.
There's no rush and no wrong answer — the frame is just there to help you start. Your work autosaves on this device.
