Regulation StationMADE BY MATT

For staff

Regulation Station is a calm-corner aid for short, low-demand resets — usually 2–5 minutes, then a gentle return to learning. Let the pupil drive; sitting alongside quietly often helps more than talking.

  • Nothing is recorded. Whatever a pupil taps is forgotten when they leave the screen — there is no log for the next pupil (or anyone) to find.
  • Breathe works well on the projector for a whole-class settle after break.
  • Still mode 〜 calms the animation right down; Dusk 🌙 softens the screen for low-light spaces. Sounds are generated on the device and start silent until turned up.
  • This is a settling tool, not support in itself. A pupil in real distress needs an adult, not an app — follow the usual safeguarding route.
