How to use Evidence Binder

  1. ⚙ Setup first — add your unit with its AQA code, paste the outcomes from the spec one per line, and paste your class list.
  2. 📷 Capture — photograph the work, tick the outcomes it shows and the pupils it belongs to (group work can tag several), add a note, save.
  3. ✅ Coverage shows pupils × outcomes at a glance — red cells are gaps to plug before submission.
  4. 📄 Portfolios builds a print-ready PDF per pupil: cover, outcome checklist, dated evidence pages.
  5. 💾 Back up from Setup regularly — everything lives only in this browser until you do.
Safeguarding note: photos stay on this device — nothing is uploaded anywhere. Treat the device and backups with the same care as a paper portfolio.
