How to use Exit Ticket & Quick Marks

  1. Type a question (or leave it blank) and choose how pupils answer — thumbs, a 0–5 confidence scale, or A–D choice.
  2. Start, then tap a response for each pupil as they answer — or hand the device round.
  3. Tap 📊 See results for an instant bar chart of the whole class's understanding.
  4. Use it to decide: move on, or go over it again? 📷 Save snapshot keeps a picture for your records.
No names and no individual data — just the class picture. Nothing is stored once you close the page.
