How to use Flashcards & Quiz

  1. Make a deck and add cards — a term and its meaning. Use 📋 Paste many to add a whole list at once.
  2. 🔁 Flashcards shows a card; tap to flip, then say how well you knew it. Tricky cards come back sooner.
  3. ✅ Quiz me builds a multiple-choice test from your deck and marks it as you go.
  4. Make decks for any subject — or get pupils to make their own; that's great revision in itself.
No pressure and no leaderboard — just your own progress. Everything saves on this device.
