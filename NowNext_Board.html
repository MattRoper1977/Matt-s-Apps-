How to use the Now / Next board

  1. Tap a card to choose what it shows — pick a quick activity or type your own and choose a picture.
  2. Now is happening now; Next comes after. Switch to 3 to add a Then card.
  3. When the current activity finishes, tap ✓ Now is done — everything shuffles along and a tick celebrates.
  4. ⛶ Full screen is great on a class display or a pupil's desk.
Clear, predictable structure helps reduce anxiety around transitions. The board remembers its cards on this device.
