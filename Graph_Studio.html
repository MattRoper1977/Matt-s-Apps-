How to use Graph & Data Studio

  1. Pick a chart type. Bar and pie suit categories; line and scatter suit measurements over something.
  2. Type your title and axis labels — examiners love properly labelled axes with units.
  3. Fill the table: a label and a number per row. The chart updates instantly.
  4. Read what the numbers say — mean, range and more, worked out for you.
  5. ⬇ Save chart downloads a PNG to drop into your write-up.
