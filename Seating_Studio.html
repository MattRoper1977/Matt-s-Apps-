How to use Seating Plan Studio

  1. Add your class list (👤 Pupils) and choose a desk layout (▦ Desks).
  2. Tap a pupil in the tray, then tap a desk to seat them — or drag them onto a desk.
  3. Set ⚠ Keep apart pairs; desks flash red if those two end up side by side.
  4. 🎲 Auto-fill seats everyone randomly (respecting keep-apart where it can).
  5. ⬇ Save downloads your plan as a picture to print or display.
