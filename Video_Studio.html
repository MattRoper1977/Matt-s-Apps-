How to use Video Studio

  1. ➕ Add clips & photos — they line up on the timeline. Tap one to trim it, change its filter, sound or framing. Photos can slow-zoom.
  2. The round button between clips sets the transition: ✂ cut → ⬩ dissolve → ⬛ fade-black.
  3. 🅣 Text adds captions at the playhead — use ⏱ Start/End here to time them.
  4. ♫ Audio adds a music track (with fades) and records voiceover from the playhead. Music automatically dips under your voice.
  5. ⚙ Film sets quality, shape (16:9, vertical, square) and film fades. Export ⬇ records the film to a video file — all on this device.
Tip: 📷 grabs the current frame as a picture — handy for thumbnails and display work.
