How to make a comic

  1. Pick a ▦ Layout (2, 3, 4 or 6 panels). Tap a panel to select it.
  2. Give the panel a background: 🖼 Photo imports a picture, 🎨 Colour sets a flat colour.
  3. Add 💬 speech, 💭 think, 💥 shout bubbles, captions, text and 😀 stickers. Drag them anywhere; double-tap a bubble to edit the words.
  4. Give a speech bubble a pointing tail so readers know who's talking. Drag the blue corner to resize.
  5. ⬇ Export saves your finished comic as a PNG or PDF.
Your comic autosaves on this device. Photos you add live inside the comic — nothing is uploaded anywhere.
