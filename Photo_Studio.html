How to use Photo Studio

  1. Add photos — each one stays a separate piece you can move, resize and spin (drag the corner ◼ to resize, the ⟳ handle to rotate).
  2. With a photo selected, use the sliders and Filters on the right, crop it into a shape (circle, star, heart…), ▭ Trim to keep just part of it, or ✂ Cut out to draw around the bit you want — perfect for photomontage.
  3. Stickers and Captions are movable too. Double-tap a caption (or use ✎ Edit) to change the words.
  4. Draw and Erase paint on a layer over the top.
  5. Drag empty space to move around; pinch or scroll to zoom. Undo ↩ fixes mistakes. Save ⬇ when you're done.
Tip: Arrange ⬆⬇ changes which photo sits on top. Photos never leave this device.
