How to use Art Studio

  1. Pick a tool on the left — Brush, Pencil, Marker, Spray, Fill, Shapes, Stamp or Text.
  2. Choose your colour, size and opacity on the right.
  3. Turn on Symmetry for mirror or kaleidoscope patterns.
  4. Layers let you build a picture in parts — add, hide 👁, lock 🔒 or reorder them.
  5. Trace photo drops a picture on a locked layer to draw over. Hide it before saving, or lower its opacity.
  6. Made a mistake? Undo ↩ or use the Eraser. Save ⬇ exports your picture.
Eyedropper: the Pick tool copies any colour already on your canvas. Pan tool (or two fingers) moves the view when zoomed in.
