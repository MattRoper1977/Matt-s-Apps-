How to use Typing Tutor

  1. Pick a level — start with the home row (the keys your fingers rest on).
  2. Type what you see. Correct letters turn green, mistakes turn red.
  3. The on-screen keyboard lights up the next key, colour-coded by which hand to use. Turn it off once you're confident.
  4. Finish the line to see your words-per-minute and accuracy.
No pressure and no leaderboard — just your own progress. Practise a few lines a day and watch your speed climb.
