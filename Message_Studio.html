How to use Message & Email Studio

  1. Pick what you're writing — emailing a teacher, replying to a college, asking for help, and so on.
  2. Choose email or message and fill in a few details. A polite, well-structured draft builds itself.
  3. Watch the checklist tick off the things a good message needs: a greeting, a clear point, a polite sign-off.
  4. 📋 Copy or ✎ Edit by hand to make it yours, then paste it into your real email or messaging app.
This tool helps you say things clearly and politely. It never sends anything — you're always in control. Tricky conversations (saying sorry, asking for help, making a fair complaint) are exactly what it's here to practise.
