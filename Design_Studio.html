How to use Design Studio

  1. ✦ New gives you templates — Certificate, Poster, Worksheet header — or a blank page.
  2. Tap things to select them: drag to move, corner ◼ to resize, side handles to stretch, ⟳ to rotate. Magenta snap guides appear when things line up.
  3. Text: tap the canvas to type. Pick fonts, alignment, bold/italic on the right. Shapes: pick one, tap to place, then set fill and outline.
  4. Keep {{name}} in a certificate and Save ⬇ can print one per pupil — paste your class list and it builds a multi-page PDF.
  5. Save as PNG for screens and displays, or PDF for printing.
Tip: arrow keys nudge the selected item into place. Everything stays on this device.
