How to use Audio Studio

  1. 🎙 Record — big red button, pause if you need to, stop to keep the take. The meter shows your level. Import audio files here too.
  2. ✂ Editor — drag across the waveform, then trim, auto-trim silence, normalise, add fades, set volume, and export a WAV.
  3. 🎛 Soundboard — nine pads, keys 1–9. Fill them from takes or the editor; they're remembered on this device.
  4. 📊 Noise — a calm green/amber/red volume display for the classroom. Big screen mode for the projector.
  5. ⏺ Screen recording makes narrated teaching clips (.webm) ready for Video Studio.
