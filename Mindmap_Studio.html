How to use Mind-Map Studio

  1. ＋ Idea adds a bubble. Drag bubbles to move them around.
  2. Tap a bubble to select it; the + underneath adds a new idea joined to it (great for branches).
  3. Double-tap a bubble to rename it; the small × deletes it.
  4. 🔗 Link two then tap two bubbles to connect them. 🎨 Colour changes the selected bubble.
  5. ⬇ saves your mind-map as a picture for revision or planning.
