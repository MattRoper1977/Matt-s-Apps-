How to make a film

  1. Set your scene. Move your model a tiny bit, tap ● Capture. Repeat. Small moves = smooth film.
  2. The 👻 onion skin shows a ghost of your last frame so you can line up the next move. ⟳ Test last second checks how it's flowing.
  3. Tap a frame in the strip to review it — move it, ⧉ Hold it (doubles it, great for pauses), or delete it.
  4. 12fps is smooth, 6fps is chunky and charming. At 12fps you need 12 captures per second of film.
  5. ⬇ Export saves a .webm — drop it straight into Video Studio for titles, music and credits.
Your frames are saved on this device automatically, so a film can grow across several lessons. ✦ New clears it — export first!
